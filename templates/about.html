Our Mission and Technology

Bringing advanced AI screening to eye health.

The Vision Behind EyeCare AI

Our primary mission is to democratize **early eye disease detection**. Millions suffer from preventable vision loss due to late diagnosis of conditions like Glaucoma and Diabetic Retinopathy. We aim to provide a **fast, accessible, and supplementary screening tool** powered by cutting-edge machine learning.

üî¨ Deep Learning Model (The Core)

The heart of our system is a custom-trained **Convolutional Neural Network (CNN)**. This model is trained on thousands of retinal images to accurately identify subtle visual markers associated with:

  • **Glaucoma:** Damage to the optic nerve.
  • **Cataract:** Clouding of the eye lens.
  • **Diabetic Retinopathy:** Damage to retinal blood vessels due to diabetes.
  • **Myopia:** Excessive elongation of the eyeball (nearsightedness).
